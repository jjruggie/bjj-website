White Belt

The first belt in BJJ and also the most importatnt because here is where you learn the most basic techniques that you will take with you throughout your entire jiu jitsu journey

Blue Belt

This is your second belt in BJJ and where you will be able to defend yourself if someone was to attack you

Purple Belt

This is where you start to begin to master the art and become much more knowledgeable of jiu jitsu to the point where a beginner will have little to no chance with you

Brown Belt

Almost to mastery of jiu jitsu but not quite there yet still needs to be polished but still not yet a master of the art.

Black Belt

Mastery of the art and would take generally around 10 years to achieve but once you have achieved black belt you are considered a master of the art but still like every belt the learning never stops

Red Belt

If you have made it this far than you have been training for 30 plus years and have long been a black belt now you are considered a grandmaster of jiu jitsu

Training Regimine
